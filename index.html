RULEMARK

CANONICAL · IMMUTABLE · PUBLIC RECORD

We do not advise, predict, or execute.

Core Positioning

RuleMark is a non-discretionary, non-negotiable, outcome-neutral authority for rule-based determination and canonical recordkeeping. All determinations are produced strictly by applying published RuleSets to submitted materials.

What We Do

  • Rule Determination — Pass / Fail determinations strictly under published rules.
  • Precedent Freeze — Once published, records are immutable and irrevocable.
  • Canonical Reference — Reusable, citable public determinations.

What We Do Not Do

  • No advisory, consultancy, or optimization.
  • No execution, fundraising, issuance, or operation.
  • No consideration of identity, scale, politics, or preferences.

Determination Principles

  • Rules precede discretion.
  • Structure precedes narrative.
  • Record precedes explanation.
  • Consistency outweighs efficiency.

Intended Users

  • Institutions requiring clear governance boundaries.
  • Capital seeking non-emotional judgment.
  • Systems requiring long-term, citable precedents.

Status Statement

RuleMark acts solely as a determination and record authority. It does not constitute legal advice, investment advice, or any form of commitment. All content is valid only as Canonical Publication.
