Core Positioning

RuleMark is a non-discretionary, non-negotiable, non-outcome-oriented authority for rule-based determination and canonical recordkeeping. All conclusions are derived exclusively from published RuleSets and apply only consistency checks to submitted materials.

What We Do

  • Rule Determination — Issue Pass / Fail determinations strictly under published RuleSets.
  • Precedent Freeze — Once published, determinations are immutable and non-retractable.
  • Canonical Source — Provide reusable, citeable determinations for capital, institutions, and systems.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not provide consultation, advice, or optimization.
  • We do not participate in execution, fundraising, issuance, or operations.
  • We do not consider identity, scale, influence, or third-party preferences.

Determination Principles

  • Rules precede discretion
  • Structure precedes narrative
  • Record precedes explanation
  • Consistency outweighs efficiency

Intended Users

  • Institutions and systems requiring clear governance boundaries
  • Capital allocators seeking de-emotionalized judgment
  • Structures requiring long-term, citeable precedents

Documents & Publications

  • Canonical Precedents
  • RuleSets
  • Freeze & Change Control Statements

Status Statement

RuleMark acts solely as a determination and record authority. It does not constitute legal advice, investment advice, or any form of commitment. All content is valid only as Canonical Publication.
